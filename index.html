🎤 Meet Shelly

Shelly Cool is a proud New Yorker — a singer, songwriter, actress, and all-around vibe curator. Known for soulful tracks like "Weekend" and "Champagne", she brings together old-school heart and fresh energy.

Her sound flows through the smooth currents of R&B, the depth of jazz, and the emotional soul of neo soul. Influenced by legends like Whitney Houston and Luther Vandross, and modern greats like Jill Scott, Maxwell, and Lauryn Hill, Shelly’s music is both timeless and uniquely her own.

With spiritual roots and a richly diverse background, she brings authenticity and elegance to every note, blending storytelling and soul into a style that stands out online and on stage.

Beyond the mic, Shelly is a savvy digital marketer, experienced brand ambassador, and a rising influencer who knows how to connect with people — heart first.

To All My FANtastic People

By Shelly Cool Singer | July 25, 2025

The world keeps shifting — trends come and go — but music? My music stays rooted in soul, truth, and that Brooklyn-born bounce.

As a Black woman in the entertainment game, I’ve had to break barriers, dodge nonsense, and find my own way — and I did it with rhythm and resilience. I didn’t come up through fancy programs or private lessons. I came up through life — from empty pockets to full notebooks, from dreams to demos.

Some of it came naturally. Some I had to fight for. But every note I sing, every word I write, every move I make — it’s all me. Real. Raw. No filter.

In this digital age where voices get cloned and artists get overlooked, I’m still here. Unbothered. Unbought. Unapologetically Shelly Cool.

“I learned how to shine even when the lights weren’t on me.”

I’m not just making music — I’m building moments. And if you’ve been rocking with me, know this: you’re not just a fan. You’re family. Stay with me. The journey’s just getting started.

— Shelly Cool Singer
